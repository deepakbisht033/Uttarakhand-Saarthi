Your Guide to Getting Around Uttarakhand

Explore Uttarakhand with Ease

Uttarakhand Bus Services

The state-run bus services connect major towns and remote areas of Uttarakhand. Comfortable and affordable, buses are an excellent way to travel long distances within the state.

Price: Starting ₹200 for inter-city travel

Private Taxis

Private taxis are available for local sightseeing, transfers between towns, and long-distance travel. They offer flexibility and comfort for tourists.

Price: ₹1,500–₹3,500 for a full-day tour (depending on the destination)

Jeep Safari

For adventure lovers, jeep safaris are a thrilling way to explore the rugged terrain of Uttarakhand. Popular in Jim Corbett National Park and other hilly regions.

Price: ₹2,000–₹4,000 for a safari tour

Train Services

Trains connect major towns such as Dehradun, Haridwar, and Nainital. This is a convenient option for long-distance travel to and from Uttarakhand.

Price: ₹150–₹500 for a one-way ticket (depending on distance)
