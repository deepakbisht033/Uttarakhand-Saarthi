Travel Tips for Uttarakhand

Make your journey safe, enjoyable, and meaningful

Uttarakhand is a land of diverse landscapes—from spiritual plains to snow-covered peaks. Follow these essential travel tips to enhance your visit and stay safe while respecting nature and culture.

Clothing & Weather

  • Pack warm layers, especially for hill stations.
  • Carry rain gear during monsoon (July–Sept).
  • Wear comfortable walking shoes for treks and temple visits.

Health & Safety

  • Carry basic medicines and a first aid kit.
  • Drink bottled or purified water.
  • Get acclimatized before high-altitude trekking.

Transport & Roads

  • Start early to avoid landslide-prone areas.
  • Hire registered taxis or use UTC buses.
  • Use local shared jeeps in remote areas.

Culture & Respect

  • Remove shoes before entering temples.
  • Respect local customs and dress modestly.
  • Avoid loud music or disruptive behavior at spiritual sites.

Eco Responsibility

  • Do not litter—carry waste back if needed.
  • Avoid plastic bags; use reusable bottles and bags.
  • Respect wildlife and stay on marked trails.

Connectivity & Cash

  • Keep some cash—ATMs may not be available in remote areas.
  • Mobile network may be weak in the hills.
  • Inform family before heading to remote zones.
