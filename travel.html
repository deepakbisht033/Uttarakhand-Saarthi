How to Reach Uttarakhand

Explore various travel options to visit this beautiful Himalayan state

Start Your Journey to the Himalayas

By Air

Uttarakhand has two major airports: Jolly Grant Airport (Dehradun) and Pantnagar Airport (near Nainital). Regular flights connect these airports to Delhi and other major Indian cities.

Tip: Book flights early during peak seasons (March–June, Sept–Nov).

By Train

Major railheads in Uttarakhand include Haridwar, Dehradun, Kathgodam, and Rishikesh. These stations are well-connected to Delhi, Lucknow, Kolkata, and other major cities.

Recommended: Overnight trains from Delhi to Kathgodam for visiting Nainital region.

By Road

Uttarakhand is well-connected by a network of highways and state roads. Buses, taxis, and self-drive options are available from cities like Delhi, Chandigarh, and Lucknow.

Popular Routes: Delhi to Nainital (7 hrs), Delhi to Haridwar (6 hrs), Delhi to Mussoorie (8 hrs).
